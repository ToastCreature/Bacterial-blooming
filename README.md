# Bacterial-blooming
This mod brings iron bacteria, and all that yu can do with it, into Vintage Story!
[Please do not steal my code or assets for AI training data or personal use without asking me first.]

Below is a detailed guide for what's in the mod, and how to use it!
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Iron bacteria- a new randomly spawning surface cover that spawns in and around certain water sources in certain areas. These can then be collected in any water tight pot, such as bowls food pots, and buckets for 1u (unit) of iron bacteria.

Dried iron bacteria- if you leave out iron bacteria in a non-airtight container for 5-14 in-game days, it will dry into dried iron bacteria, which can then be picked up for iron bacteria dust.

Iron bacteria dust- after the processes stated above, you can then take this iron bacteria dust and smelt it in a primitive furnace to get nugget blooms.

primitive furnace- a red/blue clay predecessor to the bloomery, which can be used to make nugget blooms, and reheat partially-filled containers such as crucibles or molds, guaranteeing re-use.

Nugget bloom- these are smaller variants of regular blooms, providing between 5-20 nuggets of iron after processing, depending on the amount of iron bacteria dust put into the furnace, with a conversion of 1u of bacteria to 5 nuggets of iron.

Iron nuggets- work the same as any other iron nuggets, being further processed into blooms using the bloomery, or forged straight into nuggets if paired with the "Toast's perma-workables mod"

Toast's perma-workables mod- a separate project that allows anything with logically enough metal to be added as a work material. such as nuggets, toolheads, unfinished work items, and even iron blooms (all slag needs to be removed to add the rest of the metal though)
